Who I Am

Welcome to my application for the Emerging Technology in Business and Design Major. My name is Mya Dimacchia and I am excited to share my passion for digital marketing with you!

Why I Want to Enroll

Over the past year, I have made it my mission to discover my passion. My goal has been to seize as many opportunities as possible while exploring courses that spark my interest. Since high school, I have known that I want to integrate Marketing into my future career, however my exact path remained unclear. Being inherently creative and finding joy in art and design, I was thrilled when I discovered the AMAE program (Arts Management & Arts Entrepreneurship) last year. It seemed like the perfect way to leverage my creative skills alongside business expertise. I thought I finally discovered the right program for me. However, as some time went on, I sensed that something was still missing for me. During J-term, I took the time to reflect upon myself. I recognized that I had a desire delve deeper into subjects beyond what the AMAE program offered. Despite working on logo design, I realized I lacked an understanding of the technology involved in digital artwork. It became apparent to me that I have the aspiration to advance my skills and learn more about what it takes to work in an emerging, digital world. After researching, I came across ETBD here at Miami University. I immediately knew this was the missing puzzle piece I needed in my college career. Without hesitation, I reached out and enrolled in two classes in this program, IMS 254 and IMS 222. Both of these courses have been wonderful. I never anticipated learning how to design a website, let alone create the code that powers it! Emerging Technology in Business and Design is a major that inspires me to learn valuable skills applicable to any career. With only one full month taking classes in this program, I can proudly say I have learned so much. I believe this major would be a great fit for my future career goals and I am genuinely excited that I have found my passion.

What Interests Me Most

What really drew my attention to ETBD were the intriguing courses in Design and Digital Marketing. Given my minor in Marketing, I feel that integrating ETBD with my background in Arts Management and Marketing would create a perfect combination. The rapid advancements in technology and their impact on business and design are both fascinating and inspiring. ETBD brings me pure optimism as I have already been applying these skills to my everyday life.
